Using Figma, Blender and Spline to plan and create 3D animations to enhance user experience.

Backgroud:
Motion design is the broader, conceptual process of planning, directing and animating visual elements to tell a story or enhance
user experience, whereas motion graphics is the specific, tactical and often an inforamtional result.

Definition and Scope:
Motion Deisgn is the overall discipline and process. Motion graphics is a subset  on moving graphic elements.

Purpose: 
Motion deisgn focuses on storytelling and brand messaage. Motion graphics focuses on storytelling adn brand 
message. Motion graphics focuses on communicating information such as in exapliner videos or titles.

Visual style: Motion design may include 3D and complex scenes. Motion graphics often uses 2D shapes and typography.
